SERVICES

Kindness Animal Clinic is a full service veterinary clinic and hospital. These are a few of the services we provide -

Surgery

Our doctors have extensive experience as surgeons.

Whether a routine neuter or spay or more complex surgical procedures, our doctors utilize their extensive experience in the field to provide the surgery services your pets need at a reasonable price.

Immunization & Wellness

Regular physical exams and immunizations are recommended to protect your pet from the many threats they face in the environment and to help prevent problems.

Just like as in people, preventive care can help spot problems early and allow you and our veterinarian to be proactive in your pet's care so they live a lng and happy live.

Emergency Services

When every second counts, you can count on us.

Kindness Animal Clinic is fully equipped to handle most emerencies. If possible, please call ahead so we can be ready for your arrival. PLEASE NOTE - Emergencies ALWAYS have highest priority of receiving service.

Radiology

We have a state of the art digital radiograph (X-ray) system that allows us to quickly get results.

For more complext cases, we can immediately seek out an expert 2nd opinion from a network of 60 board certified radiologists and have their results within 1 ot 4 hours depending on the urgency.

Dental Care

Regular dental care is just as important for pets as it is for people.

We provide routine cleanings, ditial dental radiographics and basic dental surgery services including extractions. Properly cared for, a pets teeth should last them a lifetime!

Laboratory

We have a comprehensive in-house lab that can quickly provide resutls for the most common conditions and diseases.

When speed is not an concern or a more rare test is called for, we utilize a lab service that provides results the next morning.
